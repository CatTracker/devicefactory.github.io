Making Of ...

What is the current landscape for producing small electronic devices?

What is it like to create a device like DF1?
  • Smart phones, now smart devices
  • Internet of things (IOT)
  • New wireless technologies
  • Rapid prototyping services : 3D printing, PCB, even assembly
  • Access to low-cost manufacturing capabilities

Electronics Design

  • Circuit design : lots of reference designs
  • PCB layout : open source tools
  • Prototyping, PCB fab

Understanding the Sensors

  • MEMS : windows to the physical world
  • Motion, temperature, humidity, light, and many more!
  • So many choices, quite overwhelming.

How to Wireless?

  • Low power
  • Optimizing range
  • Battery life
  • Custom implementation means required certification

CAD Design

  • Precision enclosures can't be 3D printed
  • 3D printing good to get an "idea", but rarely good for finished product
  • Molding cost is prohibitive
  • Lots of pitfalls and gotchas

Manufacturing

  • Need to have good documentation
  • Communication is the key!
  • Mess-ups WILL happen
  • Maintaining good relationships will help in the long-run.
